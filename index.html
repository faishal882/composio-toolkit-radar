Start where schemas repeat and credentials are cheap

Access gates beat protocol complexity

Blockers are classified from the reviewed rows. The largest groups are paid-plan/admin access, formal review, and distribution approval—not missing REST endpoints.

The agent is a research system, not a prompt

Composio discovers and fetches. Deterministic gates reject weak evidence. A human resolves the cases where names, access gates, and “official” status cannot safely be inferred.
